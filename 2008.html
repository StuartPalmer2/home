What's he up to?
 
 

2008

 

Academic
Stuart has a portfolio responsibility for academic professional development for new staff.

Stuart is an academic adviser for the Professional Development for Casual Academic Staff program.

Stuart presented a professional development session for the School of Information Systems entitled Some issues in assessment: Some examples from engineering.

Stuart made a contribution to a professional development forum for the School of Engineering and Information Technology entitled Enhancing learning experiences for off-campus students.

Stuart made a contribution to a professional development workshop for the School of Health and Social Development entitled Orienting Undergraduate Students to their Future Careers from Day 1 of their Course - some perspectives from engineering. (note - 4.4 MB ppt file)

Stuart was invited to co-present a session on e-portfolios at the University of Plymouth's Teaching and Learning Conference on 4 July 2008.

Stuart is the ITL representative on two STALGS projects for 2008:

  • Improving the level of student engagement in lectures via a mobile-phone based audience response system: a trial of first-year IT units; and
  • Appropriate strategies for improving Culturally and Linguistically Diverse Student outcomes in healthcare professional placements.

Research
