What's he up to?
 
 

2014

 

In the Faculty of Science, Engineering & Built Environment:

Mentors Faculty staff on scholarly research and publication.

Assists Faculty staff with the development of OLT teaching grants.

Assists staff in the preparation of internal and external teaching and learning awards.

Contributes to a range of Faculty outreach activities.

Faculty representative on the $3.2M OLT-funded ReMSTEP project.

On the Reference Group for the OLT-funded MathBench for Australian Universities project.

Contributes to planning and running of Faculty Work Integrated Learning units.


 Stuart retains a strong interest in Frequency Domain Analysis.

Stuart has developed methods for the visualisation and characterisation of social media interactions.

Stuart participates in the following professional activities:

  • member of the Australasian Association for Engineering Education;
  • member of Engineers Australia visiting accreditation panel member for (re-)accreditation of engineering programs;
  • member of the editorial board for the Australasian Journal of Engineering Education;
  • corporate member of Engineers Australia and Chartered Professional Engineer;

  • member of the National Tertiary Education Union (NTEU).
